High-Converting SaaS Landing Page
The proven landing page structure used by 100+ successful SaaS launches. Hero, features, pricing, testimonials, FAQ — all in one.
▲ v0 ⚡ Bolt 💜 Lovable 📋 1,923 copies
Create a high-converting SaaS landing page with these sections: **Hero Section:** - Bold headline with value proposition (max 8 words) - Subheadline explaining the benefit (1-2 sentences) - Primary CTA button: "Start Free Trial" + secondary: "See Demo" - Hero image/screenshot placeholder - Social proof: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" **Features Section:** - 3-column grid with icons - Feature 1: [Your main feature] - Feature 2: [Secondary feature] - Feature 3: [Third feature] **Pricing Section:** - 3 tiers: Starter / Pro / Enterprise - Monthly/Annual toggle (20% discount) - Feature comparison list - Most popular badge on middle tier **Testimonials:** - 3 testimonial cards with avatar, name, company, quote **FAQ Section:** - 6 common questions with accordion expand/collapse **Footer CTA:** - "Ready to get started?" with email capture Design: Clean, white background, one accent color. Conversion-optimized layout.
Pricing Table with Monthly/Annual Toggle
A sleek pricing table with toggle, feature comparison, and "most popular" highlighting. Converts visitors to paid plans.
▲ v0 📋 876 copies
Design a pricing page with: **Toggle:** - Monthly / Annual switch (animated) - Show "Save 20%" badge on Annual - Smooth price transition animation **3 Pricing Cards:** - Starter: $29/mo ($23/mo annual) - 5 projects, 10GB storage, Email support - Pro: $79/mo ($63/mo annual) — HIGHLIGHTED as most popular - Unlimited projects, 100GB storage, Priority support, API access - Enterprise: $199/mo ($159/mo annual) - Everything in Pro, Unlimited storage, Dedicated support, SSO, SLA **Each Card:** - Plan name at top - Price with /mo or /year - List of features with checkmarks - CTA button (outline for Starter, filled for Pro, outline for Enterprise) - "Most popular" ribbon on Pro **Bottom:** - "Frequently asked questions" link - "Need a custom plan?" contact link Use clean, modern design. One accent color. Shadow on hover.
Testimonials Carousel
Social proof that actually builds trust. Animated carousel with photos, names, companies, and compelling quotes.
▲ v0 ⚡ Bolt 📋 654 copies
Create a testimonial section: **Layout:** - Section title: "What our customers say" - Subtitle: "Join 10,000+ teams already using [AppName]" - 3-column grid of testimonial cards on desktop - Horizontal scroll carousel on mobile **Testimonial Card:** - Customer photo (circle, 56px) - Quote text (italic, 16px) - Customer name (bold) - Company name + logo - Star rating (5 stars) **Features:** - Auto-rotate every 5 seconds - Dot indicators at bottom - Pause on hover - Left/right arrow buttons - Smooth slide animation **Design:** - Light gray background (#F8F9FA) - Cards have subtle shadow - Avatar with green verified checkmark Include 6 sample testimonials with varied companies and use cases.
FAQ Accordion
Expand/collapse FAQ section. Clean, accessible, and SEO-friendly. One question answered = one conversion.
▲ v0 ⚡ Bolt 📋 432 copies
Build an FAQ accordion: **Structure:** - Title: "Frequently Asked Questions" - 8 questions with expand/collapse **Questions:** 1. "How does the free trial work?" 2. "Can I cancel anytime?" 3. "What payment methods do you accept?" 4. "Do you offer refunds?" 5. "Is my data secure?" 6. "Can I switch plans later?" 7. "Do you offer enterprise pricing?" 8. "How do I contact support?" **Behavior:** - Click question → expand answer with smooth animation - Only one open at a time (accordion style) - Plus icon rotates to X when open - Answer text is 15px, line-height 1.7 **Design:** - Clean divider lines between items - Subtle hover state on questions - Answers have slightly different background Include realistic, helpful answers for a SaaS product.
Hero Section with Animated Gradient
Make a first impression that sticks. Animated gradient background, bold typography, and clear CTAs.

No coding required." - Two buttons: "Start Free" (filled), "Watch Demo" (outline) - "No credit card required" small text below **Social Proof Bar:** - "Trusted by teams at" text - 5 company logos (grayscale, horizontal row) - Logos should be placeholder SVG rectangles **Bottom:** - Scroll indicator arrow bouncing gently **Typography:** - Headline: 56px, bold, letter-spacing -1px - Subheadline: 20px, lighter color - Use Inter or similar clean font Responsive: Stack vertically on mobile, reduce font sizes.
