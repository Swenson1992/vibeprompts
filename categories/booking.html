Calendly-style Booking System
Full appointment booking flow like Calendly. Service selection, calendar, time slots, confirmation.
💜 Lovable▲ v0📋 987 copies
Create a Calendly-style booking system: **Step 1 - Services:** - List of services with duration + price - Service cards: icon, name, duration (30min), price - Click to select, highlight chosen **Step 2 - Calendar:** - Month view with available dates (green) - Unavailable dates grayed out - Click date → show time slots **Step 3 - Time Slots:** - 30-minute slots, 9am-5pm - Available: clickable, Unavailable: grayed - Selected: highlighted **Step 4 - Details:** - Name, Email, Phone, Notes fields - Confirm booking button **Step 5 - Confirmation:** - Success checkmark animation - Booking summary - "Add to Calendar" (Google/Apple/Outlook) - Confirmation email sent **Admin Dashboard:** - Weekly calendar view - Set availability - View/cancel bookings Tech: Supabase, Resend for emails
