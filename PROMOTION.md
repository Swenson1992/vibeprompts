## 📝 Short Description (140字内)
VibePrompts is a curated directory of 500+ vibe coding prompts for Lovable, Bolt, v0, and Cursor. Build SaaS apps, landing pages, dashboards, and more in minutes. Simply copy the prompt, paste it into your favorite AI tool, and ship.

## 📝 Long Description
**What is VibePrompts?**
A curated library of 500+ battle-tested prompts for AI-powered app builders. 

**Why it matters:**
- No more failed AI outputs
- Prompts tested on real projects
- Organized by category and tool
- Copy-paste ready

**Features:**
- 10 app categories (SaaS, Landing Pages, E-commerce, etc.)
- Tool-specific prompts (Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor)
- Search functionality
- Submit your own prompts
- SEO-optimized (each prompt = one page)

**Who is it for?**
- Indie hackers building SaaS
- Non-technical founders
- Developers who want to move faster
- Anyone doing "vibe coding"
